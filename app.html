Our Application

You can download our application and enjoy another experience in the world of sword fencing, as the program works with you without an Internet connection.

You can see all the information about the sport, see the training centers near you, the international and local championships and their dates, see the types of swords and their prices, or even connect your sword via Bluetooth and count the correct touches and save them on the program.

You can also connect the sword to the virtual reality program and practice sports anytime and anywhere online. You can choose to play with artificial intelligence or a friend and practice sports safely, making it available to everyone and establishing the principle of equality.

Vision and mission

Fencing world Its goal is to facilitate the practice of sword fencing, make it safe and available to all people without any distinction or discrimination, for free, and enhance its spread everywhere and link it to all means of technology such as artificial intelligence and virtual reality, adding information about it, skills and training to improve the abilities and skills of the players, as well as modifying the shape of the sword and its components and making it completely wireless to make the sport. More exciting and less risky.
